// 02 — DAILY MEAL PROTOCOL

THE SCHEDULE

06:00 AM — WAKE UP

Hydration + Activation

500ml warm water + juice of ½ lemon + a pinch of Himalayan salt. Optional: 1 tsp raw apple cider vinegar. This primes gut acid, hydrates cells after sleep, and kickstarts metabolism.

06:30 AM — MORNING RUN + MOVEMENT

Fasted Cardio Window

30–40 min run / dynamic movement. Keep heart rate moderate (Zone 2). This maximises fat oxidation using stored fat as fuel. Do NOT have carbs before this — only water or black coffee.

07:30 AM — BREAKFAST

🌾 Power Breakfast — Keep your current base, upgraded

• 50g rolled oats (cooked with water, not milk) + 1 tbsp chia seeds soaked overnight
• 1 scoop Whey Protein (ASITIS 27g) mixed in or beside
• Nuts: 5 walnuts + 6 almonds + 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds (add pumpkin seeds — key for DHT-related hair loss)
• Add: ½ tsp turmeric + small pinch of black pepper on oats (anti-inflammatory, bioavailability booster)
• Add: 1 tsp flaxseed powder stirred in (omega-3, lignans for hormonal balance)
• Calories: ~520 kcal | Protein: ~38g

10:00 AM — MID-MORNING

🥤 ABC Juice — Keep it (upgraded)

Apple + Beetroot + Carrot (your current routine) — excellent. Add: 1 tsp amla powder or fresh amla if available (Vitamin C powerhouse for collagen, hair, and immune function). No sugar. Drink within 15 min of juicing.
• Optional add-on: 1 boiled egg or a small handful of roasted chickpeas if hunger hits.
• Calories: ~130 kcal

01:00 PM — LUNCH (MAIN MEAL)

🥗 Upgraded Lunch — Optimised for muscle + skin + gut

• Eggs: 3 whole eggs scrambled (keep this — excellent protein + biotin for hair)
• Swap oats at lunch → Replace with 1 medium sweet potato (boiled/roasted) OR 100g cooked brown rice OR 150g boiled lentils/dal — better glycemic + gut profile
• Veggies: Large bowl — mix of raw + steamed: spinach, broccoli, kale, bell peppers, cucumber, tomato
• Add: 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil drizzled on veggies (absorption of fat-soluble vitamins A, D, E, K)
• Add: 1 tbsp fermented food — a small portion of homemade curd/yogurt or pickle (gut bacteria)
• Black coffee: Have 30 min after eating — not with food (improves iron absorption)
• Calories: ~580 kcal | Protein: ~35g

04:30 PM — PRE-WORKOUT SNACK

⚡ Pre-Workout Fuel

• 1 banana (fast carbs, potassium for muscle function) + black coffee
• OR 3–4 dates + black coffee
• Calories: ~120 kcal

05:00–06:00 PM — EVENING WEIGHT TRAINING

Home Weight Session

Focus on compound movements: push-ups, pull-ups, squats, lunges, dips, rows. Progressive overload principle. Track reps/sets.

06:30 PM — POST-WORKOUT

🥛 Whey Shake — Critical Window

• 1 scoop ASITIS Whey (27g protein) in 250ml water
• Add: 1 tsp creatine monohydrate (3–5g) — muscle recovery, strength, and brain function (evidence-based, safe to stack with your meds — but confirm with doctor)
• Calories: ~130 kcal | Protein: ~27g

08:00 PM — DINNER

🍓 Upgraded Dinner — Gut healing + anti-aging night

• Keep whole fruit base but structure it: papaya + pomegranate + kiwi + blueberries (or seasonal berries)
• Add: 1 tbsp Greek yogurt (unsweetened) — probiotic + slow protein
• Add: 1 tsp honey + 1 tsp soaked sabja/basil seeds (gut mucilage, hydration)
• Optional: 2 boiled eggs or 100g paneer if protein target is short for the day
• No whey shake at dinner — switch to real food protein here for better gut benefit
• Calories: ~350 kcal | Protein: ~15g

09:30 PM — SLEEP PREP

🌙 Wind-Down Protocol

• 1 glass warm water + ½ tsp ashwagandha powder + pinch of cinnamon (OR take your Man Matters supplement as instructed)
• Lights down by 10 PM — sleep is where HGH peaks, hair follicle repair happens, skin regenerates, and cortisol resets. 7–8h sleep is non-negotiable for your goals.

// 05 — GUT HEALTH PROTOCOL

BUILD YOUR MICROBIOME

🦠

Probiotic Foods Daily

Curd/yogurt (unsweetened), homemade pickles (no vinegar), idli/dosa fermented batter, kimchi if accessible. These seed your gut with Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium strains.

🌿

Prebiotic Foods Daily

Oats (beta-glucan), raw garlic (crush and wait 10 min before consuming), onion, green banana, cooked then cooled rice, lentils, asparagus if available. These FEED your good bacteria.

💧

Hydration Target

Minimum 3–3.5 litres of water daily. Gut motility depends on this. Add sabja/basil seeds to water — they form a mucilaginous coating that soothes and hydrates the gut lining.

🚫

Gut Killers — Avoid

Refined sugar, processed foods, alcohol, excess red meat, artificial sweeteners (destroy Lactobacillus), antibiotics unless medically necessary. These wipe out microbiome diversity fast.

🧄

Gut Healing Stack

Morning ACV (apple cider vinegar, 1 tsp in warm water) — supports stomach acid. Turmeric daily — heals gut lining. Fibre-first eating: start meals with veggies before protein/carbs.

🌾

Diversity Rule

Aim for 30 different plant foods per week. Sounds like a lot — but oats, lentils, 5 veggies, 5 fruits, 3 nuts, 2 seeds, herbs = 16+ easily. Rotate daily to maximize gut diversity.
